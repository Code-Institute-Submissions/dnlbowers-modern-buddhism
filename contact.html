How to contact the New Kadampa Tradition

The best way to contact the New Kadampa Tradition would be to look up your nearest point of contact via the center locator page on Kadampa.org.

You could either look up a class time to attend or see if they have a "World Peace Cafe" to which you can informally drop in and enjoy a coffee whilst meeting volunteers and residents of the local center.

However, if there is no community yet in your area, all is not lost. For inspiration and resources, you can follow the New Kadampa Tradition on social media pages linked at the bottom of this page or use the methods described under the heading "Ways To Connect To The Global Community" provided on this site's Community page for a more "hands-on" experience of the tradition.

Contact Me For Malta Meet Ups

Although there is no formal link to the New Kadampa Tradition here in Malta, I have studied the tradition for the past 15 years. Along the way, I have taught meditation, attended international festivals and lived in a number of their centers to the point of taking on a management role whilst being a resident.

Whilst I have taught classes on the traditions general programme, I am no longer a teacher within the tradition. I am looking to gather like-minded people in Malta so we can eventually submit the request for a qualified teacher to be sent here to the island.

In the meanwhile, I would love to hear from you. If you are interested in learning more, want an outlet to discuss what you know, or even would like notifications on any news related to the tradition via a newsletter, fill out the relevant parts of the form below, and let's stay in touch.
