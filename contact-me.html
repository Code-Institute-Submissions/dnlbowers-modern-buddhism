Although there is no formal link to the New Kadampa Tradition here in Malta, I have studied the tradition for the past 15 years. Along the way, I have taught meditation, attended international festivals and lived in a number of their centres to the point of taking on a management role whilst being a resident.

Whilst I have taught classes on the traditions general programme, I am no longer a teacher within the tradition. I am looking to gather like-minded people in Malta so we can eventually submit the request for a qualified teacher to be sent here to the island.

In the meanwhile, I would love to hear from you. If you are interested in learning more, want an outlet to discuss what you know, or even would like notifications on any news related to the tradition via a newsletter, fill out the relevant parts of the form below, and let's stay in touch.
